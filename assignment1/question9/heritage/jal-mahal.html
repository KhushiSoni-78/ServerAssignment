Overview

Jal Mahal, meaning Water Palace, is a beautiful palace situated in the middle of Man Sagar Lake.

History

The palace was constructed in the 18th century and served as a royal hunting lodge.

Architectural Beauty

The structure is a fine example of Rajput architecture with Mughal influences. Four floors of the palace remain submerged when the lake is full.
