Overview

The City Palace is a magnificent palace complex located in the heart of Jaipur. It continues to serve as the residence of the royal family.

History

Construction began in 1727 under Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. Over time, several rulers added their own architectural styles to the palace.

Cultural Importance

The palace houses museums, courtyards, gardens, and ceremonial halls. It reflects Jaipur’s royal heritage and artistic traditions.
