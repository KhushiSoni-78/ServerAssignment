Overview

Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It contains the world’s largest stone sundial.

History

Built in the early 18th century by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, the observatory was designed for precise astronomical measurements.

Scientific Value

The instruments were used to measure time, predict eclipses, and track celestial bodies with remarkable accuracy.
