About the College

The Institute of Engineering and Technology, Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, is a constituent institute of DAVV and is recognized for its commitment to excellence in engineering education. Since its inception, the institute has played a significant role in producing skilled engineers and technologists.

The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines. The academic curriculum is designed in accordance with university guidelines and is periodically updated to meet industry requirements and technological advancements.

IET DAVV is supported by highly qualified faculty members who bring a blend of academic knowledge and practical experience. The institute provides state-of-the-art laboratories, library facilities, research centers, and computing resources to support effective learning.

Apart from academics, the institute actively promotes extracurricular activities, technical clubs, innovation cells, and student societies. These activities help students develop leadership qualities, teamwork, communication skills, and professional ethics.

The vision of IET DAVV is to become a center of excellence in engineering education and research, contributing to technological development and societal progress at national and global levels.
